I like reading philosophy. That is perhaps because I like learning and reading and maybe because I often wondered about the nature and meaning of things and realities. Recently I read Plato from the book "From Socrates to Sartre" written by T. Z. Lavine, and a bit about Aristotle and Decartes.
In the past I used to read quite a bit of Camus, which is one of the most un-assuming and un-pedantic philosopher, almost poetic, but quite deep and surprising in his analysis and conclusions. Some good books by Camus are "The myth of Sysiphus" and "The Stranger".
